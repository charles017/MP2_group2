About click2work

The company was created by Abdul Aziz De Borja & Charlie Abuloc. They became friends while attending the Kodego Web Development Bootcamp and decided to apply the skills they've learned. This website showcases their knowledge in Bootstrap 5, Javascript and API applications.

Our Mission

The main purpose of our website is to build connection, to help jobseekers find their dream job and to provide employers with a wide variety of options. Our goal is to make click2work a worldwide brand. A website that would build worldwide connection
